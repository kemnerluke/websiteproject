HOW IT WORKS

    We deploy our specialized gunfire acoustic-sensing technology in communities worldwide. These sensors our primed for the specific acoutstics that come from a gunshot. Once identified the system time stamps,classifies and triangulates the location and type of gunshots. From their our experts verify the acoustics picked up our indeed from a gunshot. Once all the information is gathered and verified we notify law enforcement and first responders via our mobile app of the precise location with a full set of contextual information about the incident
